Reliance Industries Limited (RIL) is Indias largest private sector company and a globally recognized conglomerate with diversified interests across energy, petrochemicals, retail, digital services, and new energy solutions. Established in 1966 by the legendary entrepreneur Shri Dhirubhai Ambani, Reliance has played a transformative role in shaping Indias industrial and economic landscape for more than five decades.

Headquartered in Mumbai, Maharashtra, Reliance operates one of the most complex and integrated business models in the world. Its energy and petrochemicals segment includes oil and gas exploration, petroleum refining, petrochemicals, polymers, and specialty chemicals. The companys Jamnagar Refining Complex in Gujarat is the largest refinery hub globally and is known for its scale, efficiency, and ability to process a wide variety of crude oils.

Reliance Retail Limited is Indias largest retailer, operating thousands of stores across urban and rural India. The retail business spans grocery, fashion, electronics, lifestyle, and e-commerce, serving millions of customers daily through brands such as Reliance Smart, Trends, JioMart, and AJIO. By combining physical retail with digital platforms, Reliance has redefined the Indian retail experience.

Reliance Jio Infocomm Limited has revolutionized Indias digital ecosystem by making high-speed internet affordable and accessible. With its nationwide 4G and 5G networks, fiber broadband, cloud services, and digital platforms, Jio has enabled digital inclusion, innovation, and entrepreneurship across the country.

Looking toward the future, Reliance is making strategic investments in renewable energy and sustainable technologies. The company aims to achieve net-zero carbon emissions and is developing large-scale initiatives in solar energy, green hydrogen, battery storage, and circular economy solutions. Sustainability, environmental stewardship, and responsible growth are central to Reliances long-term strategy.

Reliance is deeply committed to social responsibility and nation-building through initiatives in education, healthcare, rural development, and disaster response. Through its CSR programs, the company strives to improve the quality of life for communities across India.
